Have you ever wanted to travel abroad? Well this is your chance! Join CCA students as we team up with EF Tours to travel abroad to Berlin, Prague, Krakow, and Budapest in 2021. This experience allows your student to visit historical sites, analyze European architecture, and take a journey of personal growth, then sign up for the informational meeting now! Through this information session, both students and caretakers will learn more about the opportunities and enrichment that international travel provides.
